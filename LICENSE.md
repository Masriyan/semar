## A note on responsible use

SEMAR is a **defensive, read-only** security tool. It is intended for auditing
AI agents **you own or are explicitly authorized to assess**. Using it against
systems without authorization may violate law and the spirit in which it was
built. Like its namesake, SEMAR is meant to protect — never to threaten.
